How to use YatraGuard AI
1
Sign in with Google
Click "Sign in with Google" on the right panel or in the top bar. Your Google account syncs your saved treks, health baselines, and emergency contacts across devices.
2
Choose your trek
Go to My Trek in the sidebar. Select from Nepal's major trails and set your start date and group size.
3
View your Route Map
The Route Map section shows live trail conditions, danger zones, and safe alternatives. Connect GPS to see your real-time location on the trail.
4
Sync Apple Watch for Vitals
Open the Vitals section and follow the Apple Watch pairing guide. YatraGuard AI monitors your heart rate, SpO2, and HRV to detect altitude sickness early.
5
Emergency SOS
The red SOS button (top-right, every page) gives instant access to Nepal emergency services and shares your GPS location with rescue teams and your emergency contacts.
