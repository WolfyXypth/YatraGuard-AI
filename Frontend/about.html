How to use YatraGuard AI
1
Sign in with Google
Click "Sign in with Google" on the right panel or in the top bar. Your Google account syncs your saved treks, health baselines, and emergency contacts across devices.
2
Choose your trek
Go to My Trek in the sidebar. Select from Nepal's major trails and set your start date and group size.
3
View your Route Map
The Route Map section shows live trail conditions, danger zones, and safe alternatives.
4
Sync Apple Watch for Vitals
Open the Vitals section and follow the Apple Watch pairing guide. YatraGuard AI monitors your heart rate, SpO₂, and HRV to detect altitude sickness early.
5
Emergency SOS
The red SOS button (top-right, every page) gives instant access to Nepal emergency services.

About YatraGuard AI

Built for Nepal's Mountains

YatraGuard AI was built at a hackathon by a team passionate about trekker safety in Nepal. Our mission: use AI to prevent altitude sickness deaths, rescue delays, and weather-related accidents on Nepal's trails.

What we're building
🧠
Altitude Sickness AI
Using biometric data from wearables and real-time ascent rate to predict AMS, HACE, and HAPE before symptoms become life-threatening.
⛰️
Landslide Prediction
Geospatial AI monitoring rainfall saturation, slope stability, and seismic signals. Trail danger scores updated from live satellite feeds.
🌪️
Severe Weather Alerts
Hyperlocal weather forecasting using satellite and ground-station fusion. Blizzards and temperature drops predicted hours in advance.
